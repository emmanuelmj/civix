PS 6 | Civix-Pulse: Agentic Governance & Grievance Resolution Swarm
Vision: A hyper-efficient, "Zero-Bureaucracy" system where citizen issues are resolved through autonomous AI coordination.

The Operational Gap: Public grievances are treated as "Tickets" to be closed, rather than "Insights" to be solved. Data is silos across departments (Electricity vs. Water vs. Roads).

The Multi-Agent Architecture:
The Multimodal Ingestion Swarm: A group of agents that monitor Twitter, WhatsApp, and Portal submissions. They use OCR to read handwritten letters and Speech-to-Text for voice complaints.
The Priority Logic Agent: Uses an "Impact Matrix" to rank grievances. (e.g., A "Live Wire" in a school zone gets prioritized over a "Streetlight Out" in a park).
The Systemic Auditor Agent: Performs "Cluster Analysis" to find the root cause. If 50 people complain about "Low Water Pressure," this agent identifies the specific pumping station failure before a human engineer even looks at the map.
The Resolution Workflow Agent: Autonomously assigns tasks to the correct field officer, tracks the "Time-to-Resolution," and asks the citizen for "Verification Photos" once the job is marked done.

Note to Participants: The features listed above constitute the minimum requirements for the project. However, participants are not restricted to these specifications and are encouraged to introduce additional features, advanced integrations, or innovative modules to enhance the project's depth, creativity, and market viability.
